How to Play

Tap the next lily pad to hop the green frog up to the golden finish.

Safe: green lily pads with flowers on them.

Danger: a flower floating alone (no lily pad). You have 0.45 seconds to jump off!

No skipping pads. Finger taps only — no keyboard.

There are 30 levels. Each one is longer and harder.
